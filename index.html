PongShot

This Game was a project i put on the backburner, PongShot is a version of pong i came up with, in which you have to dodge instead of catch.
You use your arrow keys to move left and right and you shoot rockets at your opponent, rockets can cancel each other out, and if you're out of rockets you'll have to wait for it to restock.

In this project i put most of my focus on creating the menu's and fully animating them, which was fun to do but took alot of time and efford.
