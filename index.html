PongShot

This Game was a project i put on the backburner, PongShot is a version of pong i came up with, in which you have to dodge instead of catch.
You use your arrow keys to move left and right and you shoot rockets at your opponent, rockets can cancel each other out, and if you're out of rockets you'll have to wait for it to restock.

About

My name is Iliass and this is my portfolio. It's still a work in progress, but then again so is my career. In this portfolio you'll find some of the projects I made in school as assignments, but you'll also find some projects I started on my own.

I am 23 years old, born and raised in 's-Hertogenbosch in the netherlands. Aside from making and playing video games, I like to ride my motorcycle, it helps me calm my mind and it energizes me.

The reason I got into Game-Development was mostly because of my curiosity, i like to break things appart to see how they work and then put them back together. So when I found out what happened behind the scenes, my curiosity turned into enthusiasm and I was taken by the process of making games.
Not long after i started watching making-offs of popular video games, I began watching tutorials on how to make your own games, and now I'm studying Game-Development and i love it.
